Demo Junior Music Festival 2004

Committee Descriptions 

Note: Committee membership is mandatory and is in addition to the required judging responsibilities of each teacher. All teachers must be available to work all day on festival day in order to participate in FestivalWare. Once a committee's required membership number has been met, it will be closed.

Monitors Committee [4 members needed]

Responsibilities: Members of this committee will work together as a team to schedule, communicate with, supervise, and facilitate the duties of the door monitors. Members of this committee will receive training and direction from the committee Chair and Co-Chair.

Committee Chair as the team leader and team supervisor will be the contact point for any questions, concerns, and instructions regarding the Monitors Committee. Committee Chair will communicate and meet with the festival organizer.

Committee Co-Chair will work closely with the committee Chair and will assist with all duties.

  [Top of Page]

Judging Committee [3 members needed]

Responsibilities: Members of this committee will work together as a team to communicate with, train, supervise, and facilitate the duties of the judges. All teachers that have students entered in the FestivalWare are required to judge, which is not to be confused with Judging Committee membership. Members of this committee will receive training and direction from the committee Chair.

Committee Chair as the team leader and team supervisor will be the contact point for any questions, concerns, and instructions regarding the Judging Committee. Committee Chair will schedule judges and will communicate and meet with the festival organizer.

Facilities Committee [8 members needed]

Responsibilities: Members of this committee will work together as a team to organize the physical needs of the festival facilities. These responsibilities include but are not limited to creating and posting maps and directional information, setting up, and tearing down on festival day. Members of this committee will receive training and direction from the committee Chair and Co-Chair.

Committee Chair as the team leader and team supervisor will be the contact point for any questions, concerns, and instructions regarding the Facilities Committee. Committee Chair will communicate and meet with the festival organizer.

Committee Co-Chair will work closely with the committee Chair and will assist with all duties.

Responsibilities: Members of this committee will work together to record and input festival participants' scores, file ratings sheets, and prepare teachers' packets to be mailed. Members of this committee will receive training and direction from the committee Chair and Co-Chair.

Committee Chair as the team leader and team supervisor will be the contact point for any questions, concerns, and instructions regarding the Auditing Committee. Committee Chair will communicate and meet with the festival organizer.

Committee Co-Chair will work closely with the committee Chair and will assist with all duties.

Event Committee [10 members needed]

Responsibilities: Members of this committee will work together as a team to troubleshoot, facilitate, answer questions, work the front information table, direct hallway traffic, and act as a substitute, if needed, in any area on festival day. Members of this committee will receive training and direction from the committee Chair and Co-Chair.

Committee Chair as the team leader and team supervisor will be the contact point for any questions, concerns, and instructions regarding the Event Committee. Committee Chair will communicate and meet with the festival organizer.

Committee Co-Chair will work closely with the committee Chair and will assist with all duties.

Awards Committee [3 members needed]

Responsibilities: Members of this committee will work together as a team to order and distribute all trophies, certificates, and medallions. Members will also be available to teachers to follow up on and troubleshoot any problems with participants' awards. Members of this committee will receive training and direction from the committee Chair.

Committee Chair as the team leader and team supervisor will be the contact point for any questions, concerns, and instructions regarding the Awards Committee. Committee Chair will communicate and meet with the festival organizer.

Validation Committee [2 members needed]

Responsibilities: Members of this committee will verify student entries and will communicate with teachers regarding any student registration discrepancies. Members of this committee will receive training and direction from the committee Chair.

Committee Chair as the team leader and team supervisor will be the contact point for any questions, concerns, and instructions regarding the Validation Committee. Committee Chair will communicate and meet with the festival organizer.

Organization Committee [2 members needed]

Responsibilities: Members of this committee will work together to plan and carry forward FestivalWare 2005. Members of this committee will receive training and direction from the FestivalWare Organizer and will serve as Festival Co-Chairs.
