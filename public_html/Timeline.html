1. Register your studio.

September 15 - December 15, 2003

Teacher/studio registration is the first step you will take to register online for the Demo Junior Music Festival 2004. Teacher/studio registration will be available and required between September 15 and December 15, 2003. After December 15, this registration access will be CLOSED! There will be no hardcopy (paper) registration available. Registration is available only online. If you do not have a computer, the public library has computers for your use at no charge. Go to www.lvjrfest.org for teacher/studio registration. Check the website, www.lvjrfest.org and the monthly DMTA newsletter for more information.

2. Pay teacher federated registration fees.

After registering your studio, you will send a check or money order made to "DMTA" in the amount indicated on your online registration page. Send via regular mail postmarked by December 20, 2003 to:

Claudia Bessette Goetz Demo Junior Music Festival 2004 8061 Petunia Flower Way Las Vegas, NV 89147-7434

  [Top of Page]

3. Receive confirmation.

After your federated registration check has been received you will receive confirmation of receipt and committee assignment via email. You will be contacted by your committee chairperson.

4. Register your students.

January 1-February 29, 2004.

After your federated registration fees have been received and you have received confirmation via email, you may access Student Registration from January 1 to February 29, 2004.  For this step, you will need all of your students' information such as name, address, event(s) they are entering, playing level, repertoire selection, length of time needed for their selection, theory level, willingness to help serve on festival day, etc. Check the website, www.lvjrfest.org and the monthly DMTA newsletter for more information.

  [Top of Page]

5. Pay student registration fees.

After registering your students, you will send a check or money order made to "DMTA" in the amount indicated on your online registration page. Send check via regular mail postmarked by March 10, 2004 to:

Claudia Bessette Goetz
Demo Junior Music Festival 2004
8061 Petunia Flower Way
Las Vegas, NV 89147-7434

6. Receive confirmation.

After your student registration check has been received you will receive confirmation of receipt via email.

7. Schedule Student Festival Performance

March 15- April 1, 2004

After your student registration fees have been received and you have received confirmation via email, you will be able to access Student Festival Performance Scheduling. You will schedule your own students' festival performance times. The time slots available are on a first come/ first served basis. If you have students that will be performing in an ensemble event and are partnering with students from other studios, it will be necessary for you to work with the other teacher in scheduling your ensemble. Check the website, www.lvjrfest.org and the monthly DMTA newsletter for more information.

  [Top of Page]

8. Receive your students' performance schedules

You will receive your students' performance schedules and all pertinent festival day information via email no later than April 10, 2004

9. See you at festival on April 24, 2004!

Certificates and adjudicator sheets will be provided to judges and already printed with students' information.

10. Receive student awards.

Award and certificate information will be available after May 21, 2004. Check the website, www.lvjrfest.org and the monthly DMTA newsletter for more information.
